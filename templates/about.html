About

Have you ever wondered what separates a good quality Wine from an average quality Wine ? What goes in the Wine; giving it that tart, sweet and fruity taste ? Introducing Goûtons Voir© (an expression in French meaning: "Let's Taste"), your virtual Wine Expert crafted specifically to predict thereby enhance the quality of the Wine.


Goûtons Voir© employs state-of-the-art Machine Learning and A.I. technologies to mimic the expertise of an actual Wine expert and predict the quality of a Wine based on it's chemical composition. The concept of a virtual Wine expert came into existence after combining the domain knowledge pertaining to Vinification with Data Science techniques.
